# 7-Day Madeira Itinerary

## Day 1: Funchal & Cabo Girão
- Funchal Cable Car
	- Take the cable car for panoramic views of Funchal.
- Monte Palace Tropical Garden
	- Explore beautiful gardens and exotic plants.
- Funchal Farmers' Market
	- Visit the local market for fresh produce and crafts.
- Funchal Old Town (Zona Velha)
	- Wander the historic streets and enjoy local cuisine.
- Cabo Girão (viewpoint, cable car, Fajas de Cabo Girão)
	- Experience breathtaking views from the skywalk and visit the Fajas below.

## Day 2: Pico Grande
- Pico Grande (15 km out-and-back trail, 1km elevation gain)
	- Hike to Pico Grande for stunning mountain scenery.

## Day 3: North Coast Highlights
- Bica da Cana peak and viewpoint (best for sunrise)
	- Arrive early for sunrise and panoramic views.
- São Vicente (historic center, oceanfront)
	- Stroll through the charming town and along the oceanfront.
- Miradouro do Véu da Noiva (Bridal Veil Waterfall)
	- Admire the waterfall and dramatic cliffs.
- Seixal Beach
	- Relax on the black sand beach and swim in the sea.
- Seixal Natural Pools
	- Swim in volcanic rock pools with clear water.
- Miradouro da Eira da Achada
	- Enjoy a quiet viewpoint with ocean vistas.
- Mirador Ilheus da Ribeira da Janela
	- See the iconic sea stacks from this viewpoint.
- Porto Moniz Natural Swimming Pools
	- Visit the famous natural pools, great for families.
- Achadas da Cruz Cable Car
	- Ride the cable car down to a remote coastal village.

## Day 4: Levada Walks & Fanal Forest
- Levada do Moinho (9 km round-trip trail)
	- Walk along the levada with valley views and waterfalls.
- Fanal Forest (ancient laurel trees)
	- Explore the mystical forest with centuries-old trees.
- PR6 25 Fontes hike & Levada do Risco (waterfalls)
	- Hike to beautiful waterfalls and lush scenery.
- Miradouro Trigo de Negreiros (viewpoint)
	- Take in the views of Calheta and the ocean.

## Day 5: East Madeira
- Ponta de São Lourenço (8 km round-trip hike, volcanic cliffs)
	- Hike the peninsula for dramatic volcanic landscapes.
- Prainha Beach (volcanic sand, turquoise water)
	- Swim and relax on Madeira's only natural sandy beach.

## Day 6: Santana & Caldeirão Verde
- Levada do Caldeirão Verde (12 km hike, Laurisilva forest, waterfalls)
	- Walk through lush forest to a tall waterfall.
- Santana traditional houses (triangle-shaped, thatched roofs)
	- See the iconic thatched houses in Santana town.
- Miradouro do Guindaste (ocean views)
	- Enjoy sweeping views of the cliffs and ocean.

## Day 7: Peaks of Madeira
- Pico do Arieiro
	- Start early for sunrise and hike to the summit.
- Pico Ruivo
	- Reach the highest point in Madeira with panoramic views.

---

For each day, explore the listed highlights. Many stops are short visits or viewpoints, while some are longer hikes. Adjust the order and timing as needed for weather and personal preference.
